Idea

$$ \mathrm{B}^- \rightarrow \mathrm{D^{0\star}} \mathrm{D}^- \mathrm{K}^0_\mathrm{s} \rightarrow \dots \rightarrow 2 \mathrm{K}\ 7 \pi\ 4 \gamma$$

Example: $\mathrm{D}^0$

25 decay-channels are considered
  • $\mathrm{D}^0 \rightarrow \mathrm{K}^- \pi^+$
  • $\mathrm{D}^0 \rightarrow \mathrm{K}^- \pi^+ \pi^0$
  • $\mathrm{D}^0 \rightarrow \pi^- \pi^+ \pi^0$
  • $\mathrm{D}^0 \rightarrow \mathrm{K}_s^0 \pi^+ \pi^- \pi^0$
  • $\mathrm{D}^0 \rightarrow \mathrm{K}^- \mathrm{K}^+ \mathrm{K}_s^0$
  • $\dots$
Uses 35 local features
  • kinematics in rest-frame of $\mathrm{D}^0$
  • positions with respect to $\mathrm{D}^0$
  • resonance substructure
  • signal probability of the daughters
  • decay-channels of the daughters
